# Sphyros Home Services — Site Guide

Read the master workspace guide first: [`..\CLAUDE.md`](../CLAUDE.md). It covers the
shared build recipe (Astro + GitHub Pages), the publish steps, and the domain-freeing
plan. This file only covers what's specific to Sphyros Home Services.

- **Domain:** sphyroshome.com — *confirm current host with Josh; may point straight at
  GitHub Pages (not on Google Sites like the other two).*
- **What it is:** a home services company focused on **small projects & everyday home
  repairs** for homeowners (handyman-style: repairs, maintenance, odd jobs, installations,
  seasonal/outdoor). **Not** builds or full remodels.
- **Name note:** "Sphyros" evokes the Greek *sphyra* (hammer) — hands-on, solid,
  craftsmanship.

## Positioning & voice

Trustworthy, local, friendly, reliable. These are homeowners hiring someone to work on
their house, so the feel is warmth + competence. Voice: down-to-earth, helpful,
confident. Make it easy to call.

## Likely pages / sections

- **Hero** — what you do + service area, big "Get a free quote" button, phone number
  prominent.
- **Services** — list with simple icons (repairs, maintenance, remodeling, etc.).
  *(Confirm the real list.)*
- **Why choose us** — licensed & insured, reliable, local, upfront pricing.
- **How it works** — call → quote → done.
- *(Later)* Photo gallery / before-and-after; customer reviews.
- **Contact** — quote request form + click-to-call phone.

## Selected design & locked decisions

- **Design: Option A — "Hearth"** (warm sand/charcoal + amber, serif headings). This is the
  permanent Sphyros design. Working mockup: `design-options/option-a-hearth.html`.
- **No phone on the site.** Josh asked to remove it — contact is the **quote-request form only**.
- **Positioning:** emphasize **small projects & everyday repairs**. Sphyros does **NOT** do
  builds or full remodels — keep remodel/big-project language off the site. Services shown:
  Repairs, Maintenance, Odd jobs & to-dos, Installations, Seasonal & outdoor, "Not sure?
  Just ask".
- **Voice guardrail (important):** Sphyros is run solo, but the site must read *neither* way.
  Use a neutral brand "we". **Never imply a one-person shop** ("just me", "owner-operated")
  **and never imply a crew/team** ("our crew", "our team", "meet the team", "people you'll
  like"). Keep it size-agnostic and brand-first.

- **Service area:** "St. Louis and the surrounding area" (replaces the old `[Your Area]`).

**Status:** design locked; building "Hearth" into the real Astro + Tailwind site (no phone,
